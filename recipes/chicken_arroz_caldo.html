Chicken Arroz Caldo

Description:

Ingredients:

  • Olive oil
  • Onion diced
  • Garlic
  • Fresh ginger
  • Chicken wings
  • Fish sauce
  • Chicken broth
  • Glutinous sweet rice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Green onion
  • Lemon

Steps:

    Gather all ingredients. Cook and stir onion, garlic and ginger. Add chicken wings and stir in fish sauce. Pour chicken broth and stir in sweet rice. Season with salt and pepper. Serve with lemons.
