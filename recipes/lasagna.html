Lasagna

Description:

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce and paste
  • Sugar
  • Spices/seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps:

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions below.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.

How to Layer:

  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers
  8. Top with parmesan
